## 2. Understanding of the assignment

Tiko is at a scaling inflection point: existing life-skills materials were adapted from peer-
education programmes and now need to become a **standardised, quality-assured, verifiable
model** deployable across Kenya, Ethiopia, South Africa, Burkina Faso and Nigeria — while
flexing to different ages, user types (incl. SGBV survivors) and country contexts.

We read four design tensions the model must hold at once, drawn directly from your principles:

- **Scalable** yet **standardised-but-flexible** — one core spine, contextual variation at the edges.
- **Quality-driven** and **verifiable** — completion tracked and quantifiable via the Tiko app.
- **Catalytic** — mostly once-off delivery, but designed to seed lasting peer/support networks.
- **Workforce-relevant** — competencies tied to real transitions into TVET, employment, entrepreneurship.

Our proposal is organised around your four deliverables and holds these tensions as explicit
design criteria, not afterthoughts.

## 3. Proposed approach (by deliverable)

### Deliverable 1 — Comprehensive Life Skills & Mentorship Implementation Framework
- **Contextual review** of Tiko's current materials, delivery contexts, and the specific needs
  of AGYW across the five markets — including a light rapid scan with partners/Mobilisers.
- **Implementation framework**: an operational guide for how the programme is structured,
  scaled and sustained through Tiko's partner peer-to-peer delivery model — roles, dosage,
  delivery modalities, quality gates, and the once-off-but-catalytic support design.

### Deliverable 2 — Engaging, Tailored Curriculum & Materials
- **Core competency blueprint** spanning the competencies you name — triple-threat health
  information, psychosocial support, college/transition prep, financial and digital literacy,
  scheduling and interview prep, employability and professionalism, communication, resilience —
  sequenced for workforce readiness and entrepreneurship.
- **Contextualised toolkits**: modular participant workbooks, facilitator-light guides and visual
  aids with built-in adaptability mechanisms for age band, user type and region.
- Human-centred, low-literacy-friendly design aligned to Tiko's brand and behavioural-insight DNA.

### Deliverable 3 — Assessment & Evaluation Mechanisms
- **Practical assessment tools** — short post-tests, situational quizzes and scenario-based
  reviews that measure comprehension and skill acquisition and **feed digital verification in the Tiko app**.
- **Lightweight impact measurement framework** to track longer-term relevance as girls transition.

### Deliverable 4 — Capacity Building (ToT)
- **Train-the-trainer delivery** equipping Tiko staff and partners to facilitate and oversee the
  curriculum on the ground — building internal ownership so quality holds at scale without us.

---

## 4. Monitoring & evaluation plan
- **Outcome logic**: competency acquisition → applied behaviour → transition readiness, with a
  simple results chain per competency cluster.
- **Instruments**: pre/post comprehension checks, scenario rubrics, facilitator fidelity checklist,
  and a partner-level quality-assurance loop.
- **Verification**: completion and assessment data structured to integrate with Tiko app tracking.
- **Learning cadence**: a lightweight monitoring guide Tiko can run independently post-engagement.

  educator capacity programme with a full transversal-skills measurement system — pre/post
  situational-judgment instruments, a skills-indicator database and real-time evidence dashboards.
  End-to-end life-skills curriculum design + train-the-facilitator + assessment at scale.
- **Learning through Play — national curricula, Mexico (LEGO Foundation):** national play-based
  teacher-training curriculum (40,000+ educators) and ECD programme (~48,000 children) integrating
  social-emotional skills — standardised-but-contextualised design with government-certified, sustainable handover.
- **Creative Schools (FORM, 2014–22) + NASEN national CPD (England, 45 modules / 45,000+ pros):**
  sustained **mentorship + train-the-trainer** models, including mentorship by trainers with lived experience.
- **superskills! (Learning Economy Fdn, advisory):** digital credentialing that documents and validates
  transversal skills via verifiable credentials — conceptual backbone for **app-based verification of completion**.

**SSA / EdTech-MEL evidence (PROPOSED unless noted — confirm status before citing as won):**

- **Ubongo 10-Year Impact synthesis (pan-African, 23 countries):** work alongside Africa's largest
  kids' edutainment producer; socio-emotional-skill gains, low-bandwidth multilingual African delivery.
