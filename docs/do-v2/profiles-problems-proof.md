### 1.3b — profiles × problems × proof (the /do page design map)

**How to read this as a design brief:** columns are the layers of the /do page journey, left to right. **Profiles** = the entry mechanic ("that's me" chips / slider identities). Each **Problem** row = one hook card or short-form FOMO reel. **Family** = the reveal. **Proof** = the trust layer (portfolio thumbnails per family box).

**Profiles legend**

- **P1 · Accountable Program Leader** — program directors, MEL leads, founders at foundations, INGOs, NGOs, ministries; comms/development leads reporting impact upward; ops people wanting one partner who ticks all the MEL boxes. *(Amna archetype)*
- **P2 · Research & Insights Director** — R&D, insights, and data leads at consumer-goods, edtech, and assessment companies; data scientists buried in cleaning; orgs wanting to catch up to the ChatGPT world. *(Nordic archetype)*
- **P3 · Educator-Developer** — PD coordinators at universities, networks, UN-aligned bodies (PRME); higher-ed faculty; ministries and school networks needing training at scale; inclusion leads, SENCOs, CPD commissioners. *(PPCS archetype)*
- **P4 · Community Convener** — association and foundation event owners; network/consortium leads; teams planning retreats and summits. *(ATP/press.play archetype)*
- **P5 · Curious Player** — parents, teachers, kids; curious professionals who want to play before they buy. *(Harbour archetype)*
