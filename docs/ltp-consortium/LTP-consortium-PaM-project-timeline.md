# Learning Through Play Consortium — Project Timeline (PaM)
_Detailed plan to stand up the consortium and reach a first joint bid. Prepared 2026-06-30. Companion to the consortium-bidding brief, the Mo marketing strategy, and the Garrett×Maria transcript._

## Goal & success criteria
**Goal:** turn the LTP network into a working consortium that can qualify for and respond to grants/RFPs none of us could win alone.
**Done when:** (1) partners have verbally committed; (2) a shared capability map / canonical-CV v1 exists; (3) a light governance + fiscal-sponsor path is agreed; (4) the group has assembled and submitted one joint bid.

## Workstreams
1. **Convening** — relationships, meeting, buy-in (lead: Garrett + Maria)
2. **Capability & Knowledge-graph** — partner intake → shared CV/graph (lead: Maria; w.v systems)
3. **Governance & Legal** — MOU, fiscal-sponsor path, teaming template (lead: Garrett; Education for Sharing)
4. **Marketing** — identity + outward presence (lead: Mo + Payton — see Mo strategy)
5. **Tooling & Access** — provision w.v CV/agent systems to members (lead: w.v/Biz)
6. **Pipeline / BD** — pick the first joint target (lead: Biz)

## Dependencies (watch these)
- Capability map v1 (P1) **gates** the funder deck and the first bid — it's the critical path.
- Fiscal-sponsor confirmation **gates** which US calls the group can chase.
- Identity choice **gates** the marketing launch.
- Partner buy-in (Phase 0 meeting) **gates** everything downstream — protect that date.

## Risks & mitigations (from the brief)
- **CV/experience over-attribution** → tag every reference to the org that did the work; "team experience vs our experience" framing. (Integrity + debarment risk.)
- **Free-riding / uneven contribution** → contribution to the graph is the price of membership; make it explicit in the MOU.
- **Conflict on competing bids** → per-bid teaming agreements with exclusivity scoped to that bid only.
- **Fiscal-sponsor fees (~9–15%)** → budget into joint bids from the start; confirm the rate with Education for Sharing.
- **Partner-identity ambiguity** (Press Play, Lightbulb) → confirm entities before the email goes out.
