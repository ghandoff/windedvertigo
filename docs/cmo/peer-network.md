## aspirational institutions (study their marketing)

### IDEO
- **model:** design collective within kyu Collective (Hakuhodo DY Holdings). retained brand autonomy while gaining cross-disciplinary collaboration.
- **marketing approach:** IDEO U (online learning platform) is their content-as-marketing engine. design thinking methodology becomes lead generation.
- **relevance to w.v:** their collective model mirrors ours. IDEO U is what harbour could become — a learning platform that markets the consulting practice.
- **2026 focus:** new CEO Mike Peng (June 2025). "IDEO IQ" survey of 100 largest companies — positioning design/creativity as growth enablers during tech change.
- **study:** how they turned methodology into a brand (design thinking = IDEO in public consciousness). we could do this with playful learning = winded.vertigo.

### Watson Creative
- **model:** west coast branding/creative agency (Portland, Seattle, Napa, Bend). research-first approach.
- **marketing approach:** research → strategy → creative → execution. "data and instinct" philosophy. client roster = Nike, Disney, Oregon State, Kaiser Permanente, Autism Society.
- **relevance to w.v:** their "research-first" positioning mirrors our evidence-based approach. their "without agency ego" language matches our collective ethos.
- **study:** how they position research depth as differentiator rather than academic/inaccessible. how they communicate rigour accessibly.

### Playposium
- **model:** experiential gatherings for educators interested in playful pedagogy. "playful mischief-makers" community.
- **marketing approach:** community-first, event-driven. playposiums + playbooks + thriving community.
- **relevance to w.v:** directly parallel to whirlpool model. they've branded and scaled their community gatherings. w.v could learn from their positioning and growth.
- **study:** how they turned community events into a brand. how they monetise without losing community trust.

### LEGO Foundation / Learning Through Play
- **model:** global philanthropy + research engine. partners with Harvard Project Zero, TED, national education systems.
- **marketing approach:** research credibility → partnership → global campaigns. they don't sell — they fund and convene.
- **relevance to w.v:** we could position as implementing what the LEGO Foundation researches. our PPCS evidence base + harbour platform are the practical application of their theory.
- **note:** Sarah Wolman (Lightbulb Learning Lab founder) was previously Initiatives Lead at LEGO Foundation for Learning through Play in Early Childhood. potential collaborator.
