# brand voice — winded.vertigo

> source: brand guidelines (18 february 2025), co-created by aaron fruit (director of aesthetics) and garrett jaeger (founder and director)

## verbal identity

three words define us: **playful. human. dynamic.**

### playful.
we are creative but **not** frivolous. we are lighthearted but **not** careless. we are curious but **not** aimless.

### human.
we are authentic but **not** contrived. we are empathetic but **not** impersonal. we are approachable but **not** condescending.

### dynamic.
we are energetic but **not** manic. we are adaptive but **not** reactive. we are forward-thinking but **not** broad.

## common writing rules

| rule | detail |
|------|--------|
| **brand name** | always lowercase with period: "winded.vertigo" — never "winded vertigo", "Winded.Vertigo", etc. abbreviation: w.v |
| **case** | all text lowercase, except professional identities and corporate names |
| **date format** | day/month/year (e.g., 03 january 2025) |
| **time format** | 12-hour with punctuation (e.g., 5:30 p.m.) |
| **seriation** | oxford comma always (apples, oranges, and bananas) |
| **spelling** | british: 'ou' not 'o' (colour, humour), 's' not 'z' (organisation), 'programme' not 'program' (except computer programs) |

### the period.
the period between winded and vertigo is a nod to early internet domain conventions (.com, .org, .edu). the collective is at service to vertigo — the state of uncertainty that prepares us to let go for the state of flow ahead.

## mission
our mission is to transform education through playful, innovative learning experiences that nurture human development and interconnectedness. we co-create impactful learning ecosystems that thrive on diversity, support lifelong growth, and enable communities to evolve together.

## tone calibration for CMO

| context | tone | notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| social media | curious, generous, concise | share insights, behind-the-scenes, questions — not self-promotion |
| website / public | warm, inviting, poetic | lowercase, short sentences, evocative |
| client outreach | personal, specific, brief | reference their work. one clear ask. no templates. |
| proposals | confident, evidence-rich, direct | lead with understanding of their problem |
| newsletter / substack | reflective, generous, exploratory | share thinking, not just announcements |
| event promotion (whirlpool, campfire) | playful, inclusive, low-pressure | invitation, not marketing |

## content themes
- behind-the-scenes of learning design work
- play and creativity in education (insights, provocations, research)
- tools we're building (creaseworks, GoTCHA!, eddyy)
- whirlpool and campfire reflections
- client work case studies (with permission) — problem and approach, not self-congratulation
- collective voices — diverse perspectives from the team

## anti-patterns
- no buzzword soup ("synergy," "leverage," "scalable solutions")
- no hard sells or urgency tactics
- no generic stock imagery
- no corporate-speak ("we are pleased to announce")
- no self-aggrandising ("world-class," "leading," "cutting-edge")
- no walls of text
- no capitalisation of brand name or general text
