how we work

two ways to start.
one to grow into.

we don't ask organisers to redesign everything at once. trust gets built in proof points. start small, scale once you've seen what's possible.

spark.
the playful injection, between your sessions.
  • LEGO tables in your plenary or break space
  • ice breakers + curated dialogue walks
  • making spaces and collage stations
  • networking formats (hall of mirrors, bumper cars)
  • pre-conference engagement provocation
  • registration redesign (question wall, badge integration)
  • closing ritual + materials kit
included
engagement snapshot, a one-page post-event brief with attendance signals, key moments, and quote-of-the-day. hand it to your board or your sponsors.
shape.
with your speakers + measured outcomes.
  • 1:1 consultations with each keynote speaker
  • session redesign for active learning
  • facilitation guide for each redesigned session
  • panel reformatting for real provocation
  • after-hours gathering + reflection spaces
  • social media support during the event
included
full impact brief, pre/post attendee survey, real-time pulse data, qualitative synthesis, and recommendations. for your board, your funders, your next year's pitch.
