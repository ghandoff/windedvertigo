lines become loops
every system is perfectly designed to produce the results it gets.
four scenarios. four stocks. three leverage tiers.

each scenario gives you a system with a mandate and three decisions. some change parameters. some change rules. some change the goal itself.

your job is not to find the right answer. it is to notice which kind of decision bends the system — and why.
systems dynamics leverage points stakeholder negotiation causal-loop reasoning paradigm recognition
how to play
  1. choose a scenario from the menu.
  2. read the mandate. understand what the system is doing.
  3. read each intervention. expand the detail. discuss.
  4. run an intervention. watch the trajectory.
  5. after each run, read the "why" — it names the leverage tier.
  6. after all three, look for the pattern across interventions.
