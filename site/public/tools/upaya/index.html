upaya meets threshold

upaya (उपाय) — skillful means. a buddhist pedagogical principle: adapt the teaching to the learner. the right tool, at the right moment, for the right person. not a formula — a posture of responsiveness.

our application: we've taken the principle of upaya and applied it to how we sequence toys and threshold concepts. the four pairings below are a winded.vertigo design framework inspired by upaya — not traditional buddhist categories. the tradition gives us the principle; the pairings are ours.
