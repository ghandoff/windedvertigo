how we work with the CMO
the AI CMO is not a document generator. it's an active participant in the marketing loop — it strategises, reviews, drafts, and learns from what we do.

the CMO maintains the marketing strategy at port.windedvertigo.com/strategy. this is the single source of truth — campaigns, KPIs, timelines, delegation. always current.

you ask, it answers

open cowork (claude desktop app) and ask: "what should I focus on this week?" or "draft me a linkedin post about harbour." the CMO knows your role, your campaigns, and the current strategy. it answers in context.

execute + report back

wednesday CMO review

every wednesday at 9am, the CMO automatically reviews: outreach touches, reply rates, pipeline value, content published, KPI progress. produces a digest → slack + strategy page update.

strategy adapts
