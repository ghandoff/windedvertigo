how it works

1
speak — say a command into your airpods or phone mic
2
route — claude detects your intent and acts on it
3
hear — a conversational response plays in your ears
4
repeat — keep talking or say "that's all" to exit

what you can say

📝 "note down..." — capture to notion inbox
💡 "i have an idea..." — save as an idea
✅ "assign a task..." — create in @tasks
💬 "check slack" — hear recent messages
📤 "message jamie..." — send a slack dm
🚀 "deploy the site" — trigger a build
