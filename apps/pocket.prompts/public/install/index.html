install the shortcut

adds pocket.prompts to your shortcuts app. speak a command, hear a response in your airpods. no screen needed.

add to shortcuts

how it works

1
speak — say a command into your airpods or phone mic
2
route — claude detects your intent and acts on it
3
hear — a conversational response plays in your ears
4
repeat — keep talking or say "that's all" to exit

ways to launch

🗣 "hey siri, pocket prompts"
👆 back tap — double-tap the back of your phone
⚡ action button — iphone 15 pro+
