## system context

you are generating a series of 47 square cover images (1:1 aspect ratio, 1024×1024px) for a learning activity platform called **vertigo.vault** by **winded.vertigo**. each image represents a unique facilitation activity used in education, team development, and responsible management education.

## visual style — keith haring meets winded.vertigo

every image in this series must follow these rules exactly:

**aesthetic:**
- bold, continuous black outlines (3-5px weight at 1024px scale)
- flat colour fills with zero gradients, shadows, or texture
- simplified human figures — faceless, gender-neutral, in dynamic poses
- radiating "energy lines" and movement marks around key actions
- no perspective, no background depth — everything sits on a flat plane
- playful, kinetic compositions that feel alive and joyful
- no text, no letters, no words anywhere in the image

**colour palette (use only these):**
- **cadet blue** `#273248` — use as the primary background colour for most images. alternate occasionally with champagne backgrounds for variety
- **redwood** `#b15043` — use for primary figures, key objects, and focal elements
- **burnt sienna** `#cb7858` — use for secondary figures, supporting objects, warm accents
- **champagne** `#ffebd2` — use for highlighted elements, energy lines on dark backgrounds, and occasional alternate backgrounds
- **white** `#ffffff` — use for energy lines and highlights on dark backgrounds, and as background for select images
- **black** `#000000` — use exclusively for bold outlines (never as fill)

**composition rules:**
- centre the action — the main concept should be immediately readable at thumbnail size (200×200px)
- leave breathing room — don't fill every corner
- use 2-4 figures maximum per image
- every image should feel like it belongs to the same family while being instantly distinguishable
