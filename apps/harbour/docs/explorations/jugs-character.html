what changed in v5

  • grip hole carved into top-right, no external bulge. the body silhouette is now a continuous shape with a clean right edge. the grip is a rounded-rectangle cavity (20 × 34 units, roughly twice the previous size) positioned in the upper right. about 8 units of "handle arm" material to the right of the hole — enough to read as a handle without forcing a bulge.
  • watering jug raised to translate(0 -50). the full silhouette + spout are now in the upper portion of the tile; the shower spray has clear room below it. seven streams radiate from the spout mouth with droplets at the ends.
  • boat gets a proper rim opening. an ellipse at (102, 42) with ry=7 stands in for the flat cut line — fills with a darker interior tone, gets its own ink outline, and includes a subtle light curve along the back suggesting the far rim edge. reads exactly like a cup seen from slightly above.
  • drum follows the new silhouette. same rotate + clip as before, just with the cleaner jug shape under it. the spout cut-edge stays as a horizontal ink line along the bottom.
  • eyes wiggle with the body now. eyes moved inside the .heft + .settle groups (but still outside the pose rotation group), so they participate in the shimmy and breathing animations while staying screen-upright when the jug rotates.
