10 new variations

bubbles

~10 elements

pool + 4 bubbles rising from below, largest near surface, smallest popping above. drip is submerged.

rain

~9 elements

three drops falling from above at staggered heights. drip is the pool being rained on.

dew

~11 elements

pool with small dew drops resting on its surface. calm, gentle — drip at rest after morning mist.

chain

~10 elements

three drops falling in a vertical column, progressively closer to the pool. first ripple already forming. sequential impact.

mirror

~8 elements

drop above the pool + faint reflected drop below. the pool is a mirror surface. eyes on the mirror line.

quiet

~8 elements

pool + 4 concentric ripples. no drop visible — the aftermath, or ripples from an unseen touch. very iconographic.

overflow

~10 elements

pool spilling over the right edge. a drop clings at the bottom of the overflow. "too full" / playful.

peek

~9 elements

eyes poking just above the water surface + small ripple from a drop that just landed nearby. drip is submerged and curious.

skip

~11 elements

a drop skipping across the pool surface — 3 impact marks trailing, drop in flight about to skip again. playful motion.

double

~11 elements

two drops falling simultaneously at offset positions, two overlapping ripple sets starting to merge in the middle.
