swatch sketch — v1

  • Archetype: the daydreamer. Validates "it's okay to drift and not have an answer."
  • Form: flat + soft. Hosts DB form_primary values like sheet, fabric, cloth, paper, cardboard (thin), overlay.
  • Motion vocabulary: floats, catches wind, settles gently — .float translates ±3px, .sway rotates ±2° with a 30/70 asymmetry, .softblink every 4.5s.
  • Tell: a visible diagonal crease across the body. Brand-anchored — literally the name of the product. Secondary faint crease adds texture without shouting.
  • Second signature: a dog-ear fold at the top-right corner, showing a darker underside. Reads instantly as "paper/cloth that's been handled."
  • Hem-stitch dashes along the bottom edge — subtly suggests fabric without committing to a specific material (works for paper/sail/cloth).
  • Eyes sit upper-center, not at a "head" (flat/soft characters don't have a clear head). Drifting rather than tracking.
  • Function poses here are static v1 previews. Real shape-shifting (Phase B) will path-morph between them at 400ms with an "aha!" particle flourish.
