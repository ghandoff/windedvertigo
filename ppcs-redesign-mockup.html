Reading Notes & Caveats. Live show-rate excludes on-demand viewing (recordings served most no-shows). The healthy-webinar band (40–60%) is the typical registrant-to-attendee range for a strong standalone webinar; the expected-attrition curve is week-1 show-rate decayed ≈12%/session — the series stayed at or above it every week. 9am cohorts out-registered 6pm ~2.4:1 (time-zone reach). Sentiment and thematic/PRIME coding are indicative first-pass results pending human adjudication. Certificate figures are pending (Certificate of Applied Practice open through Dec 2026). Intro-poll data: 148 responses, 130 matched to the participant spine by email (88%); 18 rows retained with participant_id = NULL for manual resolution.
Source: PPCS2026_engagement.db → Cloudflare D1 · winded.vertigo. Engagement = attendance + Zoom chat + PRME Commons + Qualtrics feedback + Miro (aggregate). Individual-level linkage: attendance/chat ~100%, Commons ~98% by name, survey 82% of named respondents.
